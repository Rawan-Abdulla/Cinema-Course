Cinema in The United Arab Emirates

" دار الحي" Emirati film produced in(2009) and directed by Emirati Ali Mustafa. The duration of it is 120 minutes. The film talks about three people: Natalia Moldovan from Romania, Faisal, a young man from a wealthy family from the Emirates, and Basu, an ambitious Gujarat taxi driver and actor, whose lives intersect in Dubai.



" ضوء دامس " Produced in 2012 and talks about a trip to the city by two friends from their simple village. Where they spend most of the trip prone on the roof of the bus that is carrying them. Moreover, during the trip, they will retrieve some memories and stories and discover new worlds and strange characters through the different stops the bus passes.



" الجن" A horror movie that was produced in the UAE in 2013. A young couple returns home and lives in an apartment in Ras al-Khaimah, UAE, and the couple believes their lives will settle down. However, they discover that their neighbors are not humans but rather a jinn.



" مالالا" A documentary film produced in the United Arab Emirates and the United States in 2015. The story revolves around the Pakistani activist Malala Youssef Zee, who was the youngest character to win a Nobel Prize at the age of 17. In addition, how she lived her life chasing her by the Taliban. Directed by Davis Guggenheim, starring Malala Youssef Zee.



" المختارون" Thriller and suspense film – Emirati. Filming was completed in 2015, and released in 2016. It talks about the year 2033; the world is witnessing a desperate conflict due to the pollution of water sources, which leads to the spread of chaos and destruction.



" دكتور بسبس " The first Egyptian Emirati comedy movie. It produced in 2019. Dr. Bisbis is an Egyptian veterinarian who does not master his profession, as he works in the circus, and causes the death of most of the animals he treats. Suddenly, he discovers that he hears and understands all the animals. He is called by a businessman living in the Emirates to treat his lion, hence surprises and events in a comic form.
