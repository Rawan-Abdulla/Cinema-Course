Cinema in Palestine

" يد إلهية" Is a Palestinian comedy film saturated with signs and symbols. It is directed by the Palestinian artist Elia Suleiman, and a co-production (French, Palestinian, Moroccan, German) in 2001, with a duration of one hour and thirty-two minutes. The film won two awards, the Jury Prize at the 2002 Cannes Film Festival and the International "screen" Award at the European Film Festival for non-European films.



" الجنة الآن "A political and psychological drama directed by Hani Abu Asaad about two Palestinian men preparing for a suicide bombing attack in Israel. It won the Golden Globe Award for Best Foreign Language Film and was nominated for an Academy Award in the same category.



" سجل اختفاء" A 1996 drama film written and directed by Elia Suleiman, an Arab from the occupied interior. This movie is Elia Soliman's first feature film. The film won the admiration over the world and won the "Luigi de Laurentiis" award at the Venice Festival in its first year, which is awarded for the best film and is the director's first work. This was the first Palestinian film to be screened nationally in the USA.



" ملح هذا البحر" A Palestinian romantic drama film produced in 2008. The film stars the Palestinian-American actress, Suheir Hammad, and co-stars the Palestinian artist, Saleh Bakri. It was nominated for many awards and directed by Anne Marie Jacir.



" الذاكرة الخصبة" A feature-length Palestinian documentary film, and is the first Palestinian cinematic film directed by director Michel Khalifi in 1980, the duration of the film is 100 minutes. The film won the Arab Critics Award at the Carthage Film Festival in 1980. This film revealed the talent of the director, Michel Khalifa, son of Nazareth.



" عرس الجليل" A 1987 movie directed by Michel Khleifi. He was awarded the International Critics Award in Cannes in 1987.



" الجدار الحديدي" is a 2006 documentary film about the establishment of Israeli settlements in the West Bank, which, the film argues, is a strategy for permanent occupation of the territory. Produced by the Palestinian Agricultural Relief Committees and Palestinians for Peace and Democracy, it was the "Official Selection" of the Al-Jazeera Television Production Festival
