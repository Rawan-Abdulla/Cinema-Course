Cinema in Libya

" أسد الصحراء" The Arabic version was called "Omar Al-Mukhtar", a historical epic war film, produced in 1981, starring Anthony Quinn, who played the role of the Libyan commander Omar Al-Mukhtar in fighting Mussolini's Italian army before World War II. The film is directed by the Syrian director Mustafa Akkad, filmed by Jack Hildriard, and edited by John Shirley, and its budget is about 35 million US dollars.



" الشظية"is a Libyan movie produced in 1986, and it tells in a dramatic story about the problem of mines in Libya as a remnant of World War II.



" الضوء الأخضر " Green Light is a Libyan movie with the participation of actors from Egypt and Morocco, produced in 1976. It talks about a family that lives in a state of duality between a father who bullying them and forcing them to completely close out of life and the desire of its members to live their life.



" جثة ناجي" It is a Libyan humanitarian drama film released in 2018, written by writer Hashem Al-Zarrouk, directed by Raouf Bayou, and starring Nidal Kahloul, Anwar Al-Ter, and Khaled Qumati.
