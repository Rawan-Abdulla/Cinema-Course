Cinema in Jordan

"صراع في جرش"The first Jordanian attempt to produce a feature film. The film was the product of the personal efforts of a group of Jordanian youth who aspire to make movies. It documents the Jordanian and Palestinian cities in the 1950s, as well as the values of freedom and liberation of usurped lands.



"وطني حبيبي" It is considered the second Jordanian feature film to be filmed in several places in the Kingdom, especially Amman, in the early 1960s. It talks about the heroics of the Arab army against the Israeli forces within a melodramatic love story. The film tells a romantic love story in which the hero abandons his girlfriend in order to perform the duty of the nation against the enemy.



" فيلم الحذاء 1989" A short Jordanian fictional film that takes place in a social context by the Jordanian writer (Muhammad Tamlia), and revolves around a poor child whose mother brings him women's shoes and a large coat to protect him from the rain, but he hides them and goes barefoot to school. He finds a committee that distributes shoes to poor students, but finds new shoes that are bigger than his feet, too.



"2007 فيلم الاب كابتن رائد " The film tells the story of a simple worker at the airport who tells the story of a group of children in the popular neighborhood in which he lives, and his adventures as a former pilot around the world, and the children were called Abu Raed as the captain of the national team. He was able to tell his exciting stories to children and boys, inspired by the old books that occupy a corner of his home. Abu Raed is a friend of the people of the neighborhood.



"2011 فيلم الجمعةالاخيرة " Youssef suffers from testicular problems that forced him to undergo a serious surgery within four days, but he cannot bear the high costs of the operation. Although he needs money and does not want to disclose what he suffers. He is forced to turn to his ex-wife, who broke up with him due to losing his job due to the global crisis, and his 14-year-old son.



"2012 لما ضحكت موناليزا" The film deals with the story of a Jordanian girl named (Mona Lisa), who did not know the smile before. She falls in love with the Egyptian reporter (Hamde). She becomes a girl who seeks freedom from social pressures and wants to become more independent in her life and improve her poor financial situation. So she can paint her life as she likes and with whom.



" 2014-2015 الذيب" The film’s story comes within the framework of a dramatic adventure film set in the Jordanian desert during the First World War in 1916. The film tells the story of a Bedouin boy named Theeb. He and his brother decided to flee their tribe after being attacked by bandits. They embark on a journey full of dangers at a time when the region is on the cusp of the Great Arab Revolt, and political events have rippled, resulting in the death of an older brother. Exposing the young boy to experiences that make him mature prematurely.



"2015 'فيلم 3000 ليلة '" Layal is a young schoolteacher who lives with her husband, Farid in the occupied West Bank town of Nablus, Palestine. They are preparing to leave for Canada in search of a new life when Layal is arrested and falsely accused of helping a teenage boy suspected of attacking a military checkpoint. When she refuses to testify against the boy in court, Layal is charged with being an accomplice and sentenced to 8 years in prison despite the staunch defense put up by her human rights defense lawyer.



"2019 فيلم طعم الطين " The film deals with the topic of "adopting children of unknown parentage", and the anecdotal events that take place in Jordan. The events revolve around a married couple who did not give birth to children, and because of infertility, they are exposed to societal violence that inflicts them psychological and moral harm, so they decide to bring a surprise to the family and society, which provoked that. Which sparked a controversy about them.
