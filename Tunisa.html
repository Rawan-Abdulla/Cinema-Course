Cinema in Tunisia

"صمت القصور " Is a 1994 Tunisian film co-written and directed by Moufida Tlatli. The film investigates issues of gender, class and sexuality in the Arab world through the lives of two generations of women at a prince's palace.



" الهائمون" Is a 1984 film by Tunisian writer and director Nacer Khemir. It is the first part of Khemir's "Desert Trilogy" that also includes "The Dove's lost necklace" and Bab'Aziz. It stars Nacer Khemir, Soufiane Makni, Noureddine Kasbaoui, Hedi Daoud, and Sonia Ichti. It was filmed in Tunisia..



" طوق الحمامة المفقود" Is a movie directed by Tunisian director Nasser Khmer. Chosen from the list of the 100 best Arab films of the twentieth century.



"ريح السد" A Tunisian drama film, released in 1986, directed by Nuri Bouzid. It was shown in the Un Certain Regard in 1986 at the Cannes Film Festival.



"عصفور السطح " Is a Tunisian film made in 1990, the first fiction feature film by the director Ferid Boughedir.
