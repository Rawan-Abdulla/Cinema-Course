Cinema in Bahrain

" حمد والقراصنة" A Bahraini American television movie produced by the Walt Disney Company in 1971. Hamad is an orphan. He decided to go with the sailors to collect pearls. While sailing, a strong storm blows, causing Hamad to fall into the sea. Other sailors rescued Hamad, but Hamad later discovered that those who saved him were pirates who stole the relics of the Dilmun Civilization. Hamad escapes them, and here the pirate chase begins.



" الحاجز " Is a film produced in Bahrain and released in the year 1990, directed by Bassam Al Thawadi. Budget and revenue The production cost of the film was roughly $ 150,000.



" الزائر" Is a horror movie, a Bahraini thriller starring Fatima Abdul Rahim, Ali Al Ghurair, Ahmed Mubarak, Amin Al Sayegh, Ahmed Aqlan, Mustafa Rasheed, Jamaan Al Rowaie, Director: Bassam Al Thawadi, Writer: Bassam Al Thawadi, Scenario: Farid Ramadan, Starring: Fatima Abdel Rahim, Ali Al Ghurair , Ahmed Mubarak, Jumaan Al-Ruwaie.



" بيت الجن" Is a Bahraini horror film directed by Hamad Al Shehabi based on a true story, shown in 1994, and its events revolve around a family consisting of Umm Rashid, Bu Rashid, Dalal, Hayat and an adult, whose house is inhabited by the jinn and they face many problems that are forced to sell the house in Hamad Town in February 1994.



" حكاية بحرينية" Is a Bahraini film directed by Bahraini director Bassam Al-Thawadi and starring a very distinguished elite of Bahraini actors such as Jamaan Al-Ruwaie, Fatima Abdul Rahim. The film tells about an aspect of Bahrain in the past, specifically from Muharraq in 1967 to 1970, and presents aspects of the current Bahraini reality such as freedom, women's issues, violence, discrimination and sectarianism, and believing in myths.



" بين بحرين" Is produced by Axir, in joint cooperation with UN Women and the National Council for Women. It was written by Maryam Naoum, the screenplay and dialogue of Amani Al-Tunisi and directed by Anas Tolba. Fatima Adel, Yara Jubran, Thara Jbeil, Mahmoud Fares, Arifa Abdel Rasoul and Lubna Wans participate in the film.
