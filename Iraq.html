Cinema in Iraq

" ابن بابل " Produced in 2009 that tells the story of a million missing in Iraq, starring Shazada Hussein as Umm Ibrahim, Yasser Talib as Ahmed Bashir, Majed as Musa, and the film is a joint production between Pyramedia's company, Pyramedia, Film Production Center (Palestine), and Sunny Land Film of Arab Radio and Television (Egypt).



" القاهرة-بغداد" Is considered the first joint Egyptian-Iraqi production, made in cooperation with the Egyptian Federation of Technicians. Filming the interior scenes began in the summer of 1946. The film was shown in two cinemas, which were the Al Hamra Cinema and the Erdrumli Cinema But the film is now considered one of the cinemas' needs.



" العودة الى بابل" Is a documentary film by Iraqi director Abbas Fadel that takes place in Iraq in 2002. The film participated in many international festivals (Carthage, Paris, Marseille, Tübingen, Biarritz ...) and it was also shown by TV channels in France, Canada, Switzerland, and Belgium.



" الملك غازي" Is an Iraqi film produced in 1993 that deals with the biography of King Ghazi of Iraq, and it is one of the few films produced during the period of the economic siege. Directed by Muhammad Shukri Jamil, Starring: Sattar Khudair, Maysoon Al-Bayati, Hana Muhammad, Youssef Al-Ani, Aziz Khayoun, Ghazi Al-Tikriteez.



" سعيد افندي " Is an Iraqi film directed by Cameran Hosni in 1957 Stars: Youssef El Ani, Zaynab. The film is based on the story of "Brawl" by Iraqi writer Edmond Sabri. The film was nominated for a prize at the 1959 Moscow International Film Festival.



" الأيام الطويلة " Is a film produced in Iraq, the novel of the long days in which the history of Saddam Hussein’s life was written under pseudonyms, written by Abdul Amir Mualla. The film is directed by Tawfiq Saleh and written by Saddam Hussein and Tawfiq Saleh, starring Saddam Kamel.



" الظامئون " Is an Iraqi tragic film, produced in 1972. The events of the film revolve in 1940 about a remote Iraqi village, whose land is affected by drought, so a group of villagers searches for a source of water other than rain, so they dig an artesian well, directed by Muhammad Shukri Jamil.



" الجابي "An Iraqi cinematic film produced in 1968, the story, script, and dialogue of Jaafar Ali, and starring Asaad Abdul Razzaq, Khalil Al-Rifai, Qassem Al-Malak, and Wadad Salem. The film takes place inside a bus carrying passengers, in which it talks about people's daily lives and concerns.
