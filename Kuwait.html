Cinema in Kuwait

" بس يا بحر" A Kuwaiti film about the life of pearl fishermen in Kuwait during the independence phase, directed by Khaled Al-Siddiq in 1972, written and dialogue by the writer Abdul Rahman Al-Saleh, which won many awards at the Arab and international festival.



" عرس الزين " Is a Kuwaiti drama film directed by Khaled Al-Siddiq. It is based on the novel of the same name. Director by Khaled Al-Siddiq, Screenplay Tayeb Salih, Starring Ali Mahdi Muhammad



" الصمت" It talks about Kuwait’s past in diving and fishing, and this film is represented by artists from the generation of pioneers. It was shown in March 1980 by: Abdul Rahman Al-Duwaihi and directed by: Hashim Muhammad Al-Shakhar, actors Hayat Al-Fahd, Khaled Al-Nafisi, Maryam Al-Ghadhban, Ibrahim Al-Sallal, Khaled Al-Obaid Ahmed Al-Saleh, Ali Al-Breiki, Salem Al-Faqaan, Basimah Hamada, Zainab Al-Dhahi, Wafa Al-Amer, Faihan Al-Aribed, Abdullah Ghuloom.



" الباب الموصد" Is a Kuwaiti movie produced in 1965AD that deals with the spread of cholera in Kuwait in 1831, which killed about 60% of the population at that time. Director: Yahya Faiq, Writer: Saleh Hamad, Screenplay and Dialogue: Yahya Faiq, Starring: Maryam Al-Saleh, Lotfi Abdul Hamid, Jasim Al-Nabhan, Abdul-Jabbar Abdul-Majeed, Abdul-Razzaq Nassif.



" معتوق بانكوك" A Kuwaiti comedy film produced by Foroughy Art Production for the year 2009/2010, written by Ayman Al-Hubail and directed by Mazen Al-Jabali. It achieved unexpected success and was shown in all Kuwaiti theaters and took its Gulf course.



" سوالف طفاش" A Kuwaiti comedy film directed by Yusef Al-Kooheji, who participated in the completion of the Sawalif Tafash series in its first season in the year 2009 as an assistant director, and then as a director in the second and third seasons.
