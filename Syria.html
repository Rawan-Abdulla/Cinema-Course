    " المخدوعون " Syrian film produced in (1972) by the General Organization for Cinema and directed by Tawfiq Salih based on Ghassan Kanafani's novel "Men in the Sun" (1963), starring Abdul Rahman Al Rashi, Muhammad Khair Halawani, Bassam Lotfi, and Thanaa Debsi, it is one of the hundred most important films in the history of Arab cinema, one of the works that come close to Kanafani's vision of the Palestinian cause, was received positively by critics and won several Arab and foreign awards.



    " نجوم النهار " directed and written by Usama Muhammad in (1988) and the actors Zuhair Abdul-Karim, Maha Al-Saleh, Sabah Al-Salem, Nahed Asi, Salwa Hanna and Karam Qudda, produced by the General Film Organization in Syria. It is banned his public performance after Hafez Al-Assad saw it and refused to comment for its shocking content that explains the reality of the Alawite sect.



    " الليل الطويل" A film by the Syrian director Hatem Ali. The film is distinguished by its boldness in presenting the contemporary Arab and political reality in Arab societies. Starring For a film that celebrated a number of the most prominent stars of the Syrian screen, including the artist Khaled Taja, Amal Arafa, Basil Khayat, Selim Sabry and Zuhair Abdel Karim and the young Tunisian actress Anisa Daoud, who won the Golden Bull Prize at the Taormina Festival.
